1. Read Widely and Frequently: Explore different genres to encounter diverse words.
2. Use a Thesaurus: Find synonyms for new words to expand your understanding.
3. Flashcards: Create and review flashcards with definitions and examples.
4. Word of the Day: Subscribe to get a daily dose of new words.
5. Practice Writing: Use new words in your daily writing tasks.
6. Play Word Games: Engage in games like Scrabble and Boggle.
7. Engage in Conversations: Use new words in conversations to reinforce learning.
8. Listen to Audiobooks and Podcasts: Hear words pronounced and used in context.
9. Join a Vocabulary Group: Share and discuss new words with others.
10. Consistency is Key: Make vocabulary practice a daily habit.
